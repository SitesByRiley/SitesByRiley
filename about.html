About
Riley

18 years old. 10 years coding. Brisbane-based and genuinely enjoying what I do.

Riley Geddes — Founder

Started young.
Still going hard.

I wrote my first line of code when I was 8. Most kids were playing sport — I was building stuff on the internet and figuring out why it broke. By the time I finished school I had a decade of real, self-taught experience behind me.

I started Sites By Riley because I kept seeing the same thing: small Brisbane businesses paying a lot for websites that were slow, generic, or just didn't work for them. I figured there was room for a developer who just did things straight.

I do things differently — I take the time to understand your business before I touch a single line of code, and I build everything from scratch, for you. You'll deal with me directly, start to finish. No account managers, no middle layers. Just someone who genuinely cares that your site does its job.

How I work

Things I don't budge on

01
Fixed quotes only
You get a price before I start. Not an estimate, not an hourly rate — a real number you can say yes or no to. If something unexpected comes up, I'll tell you before I do anything that changes the cost.
02
Built from scratch, every time
I don't use templates. I don't use page builders. I write every line of code. It takes longer but it means your site is actually yours — it won't look like a hundred other sites from the same Squarespace theme.
03
You talk to me, always
I don't have account managers or project coordinators. You deal with me directly from the first message to the final file handover. If something needs to change, you tell me, not a form.
