Booking-Ready
Website

Everything in the Business tier, plus a live booking system. Customers pick a time, pay a deposit, and get a confirmation — all without you being involved.

What makes this different?

The core difference between this and the Business Website is automation. With a Business site, a customer finds you, sees your services, and then has to call or email to book. With a Booking-Ready site, they can pick a time, see what deposit is required, pay it, and get a confirmation — all from your website, any time of day.

For appointment-based businesses — beauty therapists, personal trainers, tradies, tutors, anyone who works by the hour — this is the one that actually reduces your admin. You wake up with bookings already made while you were asleep.

What's included

  • Everything in the Business Website
    All the pages, reviews section, Google Maps, SEO basics, page transitions, 3 rounds of changes — all of it.
  • Dedicated Book Online page
    A separate page built entirely around the booking experience — not just a calendar thrown onto the contact page.
  • Live availability calendar
    Shows real available and unavailable dates based on your working hours. Closed days are greyed out, available days are clickable.
  • Real-time deposit calculator
    Customer selects their service, the deposit amount updates instantly on screen. No confusion, no back-and-forth about how much to pay.
  • Booking platform connected
    Connected to your booking system (Calendly, Fresha, Square, Timely or similar). Customers book and pay directly — no extra steps.
  • Waitlist form
    When you're fully booked, customers can join a waitlist instead of leaving. You capture the lead — they get contacted when something opens up.
  • Instagram feed section
    A grid that connects to your Instagram. Shows your most recent posts automatically — great for beauty, fitness, food and any visual business.
  • Payment link set up on request
    If you want to take actual payments (not just deposits) through the site, I can set up a Stripe or PayPal link as part of the build.

Who is this actually for?

Any business where customers need to book a time with you. Beauty therapists, lash artists, personal trainers, massage therapists, tutors, cleaners, tradies who work by appointment — anyone where the main action you want a visitor to take is "book a time."

If your current system is "call me or send a DM and I'll check my calendar and get back to you" — this replaces all of that with something that works while you sleep.
