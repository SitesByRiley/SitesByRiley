A proper multi-page site for your business. Built from scratch, structured to convert visitors into customers, and handed straight to you.

What is a Business Website?

It's a full multi-page site — the kind that makes someone think "these people are legit" within the first 5 seconds. You get separate pages for your services, your story, your reviews and how to contact you. Everything is connected, everything is fast, and everything is designed to match your brand.

The difference between this and a profile page is structure. Instead of one long scroll, customers can navigate to exactly what they need. A tradie gets a services page that lists everything they do. A café gets a menu page. A salon gets a gallery. It's proper.

What's included

  • Up to 5 separate pages
    Home, Services, About, Contact — plus one more that suits your business. All linked together with a shared nav and footer.
  • Google reviews section
    Paste in your real Google reviews and they'll be displayed on your home page with star ratings, reviewer names and everything. Nothing builds trust faster than reviews from real people.
  • Contact form & Google Maps embed
    A working enquiry form that sends straight to your email, plus an embedded map so customers can see exactly where you are.
  • SEO basics — page titles & meta descriptions
    Every page gets a proper title and description so Google knows what it's about and shows it correctly in search results.
  • Scroll animations & page transitions
    Sections animate in as you scroll. Pages fade between each other. These details make the difference between a site that feels built and one that feels bought.
  • 3 rounds of changes
    You see the build, give feedback, I action it. Three times. That's enough to get it exactly right without scope creep blowing out the timeline.
  • Files delivered — you own them outright
    Everything handed over as a folder of HTML, CSS and JS files. Free to host on GitHub Pages, or wherever you like. No ongoing fees to me.

How is it better than the Personal Profile Site?

The big jump is multiple pages. A business needs structure — customers want to navigate to your services, read about who you are, see your reviews, then contact you. One long scroll doesn't cut it when there's real content to show.

You also get a Google reviews section, which the Profile Site doesn't have. For most businesses this is the most important trust signal on the page — it's the first thing I'd look at if I was deciding whether to call you.
