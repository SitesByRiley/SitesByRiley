What is a Personal Profile Site?

It's exactly what it sounds like — a single, well-designed page that tells people who you are, what you do, and how to get in touch. Clean, fast, professional. No bloat, no unnecessary pages, just the essentials done really well.

It's the foundation of your online presence. When someone Googles your name, this is what comes up. When you hand someone your business card and they scan the QR code, this is what they see. First impressions matter — this one will be a good one.

Who is it for?

FreelancersDesigners, photographers, writers, consultants — anyone who sells their own skills.
TradiesPlumbers, electricians, painters — a professional page beats a Facebook page every time.
CreativesMusicians, artists, performers — a simple hub that links to everything else you do.
Sole tradersAny individual in business who needs a solid home base on the internet.

What's included

  • Single-page design, up to 5 sections
    Hero, about, work/services, testimonials (if you have them), and contact — all on one clean page.
  • Built from scratch — not a template
    I write every line of code. Your site won't look like anyone else's because it literally isn't anyone else's.
  • Fully mobile-responsive
    Looks perfect on a phone, a tablet, and a desktop. Over half your visitors are on mobile — it matters.
  • Contact form wired up
    Enquiries come straight to your inbox. No CMS, no login — it just works.
  • Scroll animations & hover effects
    Sections fade in as you scroll, buttons respond to hover — all the small details that make a site feel considered.
  • 2 rounds of changes
    You see the build, give feedback, I action it. That happens twice. After that, extra changes are quoted separately.
  • Files delivered — you own them outright
    You get the HTML, CSS and JS files. Host them on GitHub Pages for free, or wherever you like. No ongoing fees to me.

How the process works

You fill in the contact form and tell me about yourself. I come back with a quote and a start date, usually the same day. Once you confirm and pay a deposit, I start building. I'll share a live preview link when it's ready for your review. Two rounds of feedback later, we're done — usually within 3 days of starting — often sooner.
